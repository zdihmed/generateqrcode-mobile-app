## Information We Collect

To provide you with our services, we collect different types of information depending on how you use our platform:

### Information You Give Us Directly

- **Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password
- **QR Code Content:** The information you choose to encode in your QR codes (URLs, text, contact information, etc.)
- **Payment Information:** We use Stripe as our payment processor. When you subscribe to a paid plan, your payment information is collected directly by Stripe. We never see, store, or handle your credit card details - all payment processing is handled securely through Stripe's PCI-compliant systems
- **Communication Data:** When you contact our support team, we keep records of our conversations to help you better
- **Customization Preferences:** Your QR code design choices, branding preferences, and template selections

### Information We Collect Automatically

- **Usage Data:** How you interact with our services, which features you use, and when you use them
- **Device Information:** Your device type, operating system, browser type, and IP address
- **QR Code Analytics:** If you create trackable QR codes, we collect scan data including location, device type, and scan timestamps
- **Cookies and Similar Technologies:** We use cookies to remember your preferences and improve your experience (see our Cookie Policy for details)
- **Mobile App Analytics:** When using our mobile application, we collect app usage data, feature interactions, and performance metrics through Firebase Analytics and AppsFlyer to improve app functionality and user experience

### Analytics and Attribution Services

To improve our mobile application and understand how users interact with our services, we use third-party analytics and attribution tools:

**Google Analytics for Firebase:** We use Firebase Analytics to collect usage data from our mobile app, including:
- App opens and session duration
- Feature usage and navigation patterns
- Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
- General location data (country/region level)
- In-app events (QR code creation, downloads, feature interactions)

Firebase Analytics data is processed by Google LLC in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. Data is retained for 14 months by default. You can learn more at https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

**AppsFlyer:** We use AppsFlyer for mobile app attribution and campaign analytics, which collects:
- Install source and campaign data
- Device identifiers (Advertising ID, if available)
- In-app events and user engagement metrics
- General location data for attribution purposes

AppsFlyer processes this data in accordance with their Privacy Policy available at https://www.appsflyer.com/legal/privacy-policy/. Data is retained for 24 months.

**Your Control:**
- You can limit ad tracking through your device settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising; Android: Settings > Google > Ads)
- Disabling analytics will not affect your ability to use our core services
- For iOS users, we respect your App Tracking Transparency (ATT) choices

### Information from Third Parties

If you sign up using a social media account or integrate third-party services, we may receive basic profile information from those platforms.

## State-Specific Privacy Rights

### California Residents

If you're a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) gives you additional rights:

- Right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell
- Right to delete personal information we've collected from you
- Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (though we don't sell your data)
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights
- **Right to opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information for targeted advertising:** While we don't sell your data, our use of analytics services may constitute "sharing" under California law. You can opt-out by:
  - Disabling ad tracking in your device settings
  - Contacting us at support@generatetheqrcode.com to request analytics opt-out

### Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, and Other State Residents

If you live in Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, or another state with comprehensive privacy laws, you have similar rights including:

- Confirm whether we're processing your personal data
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccuracies in your personal data
- Delete your personal data
- Obtain a copy of your personal data
- Opt-out of targeted advertising (if applicable)

To exercise these rights, please email us at support@generatetheqrcode.com with "Privacy Rights Request" in the subject line.
